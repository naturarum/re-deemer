# Space Case TE-2 — Factory Patches

Sixteen panel recipes, drawn from the use cases in the original TE-2
documentation. Anything not listed stays at its default. Save them as host
presets after dialing in.

---

## 01 · Front Porch Slap
Tight rockabilly slap on fresh ferric.
TIME 120 ms · FDBK 18% · TAPE IN +2 dB · TP LVL 0 dB · DRY 0 dB · HPF 80 Hz · LPF 9 kHz · Tape I · MECH 15%

## 02 · Dub Shot
The filters do the talking; ride HPF and FDBK live.
TIME synced 1/4 (SYNC on, DIV 1/4) · FDBK 78% · HPF 150 Hz · LPF 2.4 kHz · RES 25% · OUT DRV 30% · Tape I

## 03 · Murk Cathedral
Long, dark, dissolving.
TIME 1.2 s · FDBK 92% · LPF 1.6 kHz · HPF 60 Hz · MECH 60% · NOISE 120% · Tape I

## 04 · Self Portrait
Feedback past unity; the tape itself is the limiter. Feed it one note, walk away.
TIME 450 ms · FDBK 104% · TAPE IN −6 dB · LPF 3.5 kHz · HPF 200 Hz · DRY 0

## 05 · Pitch Swell Cycle
The PDF's "feedbacks mildly pitching upwards over a long stretch of time".
White set ON → TM · faders 2–8 ascending 0.35 → 0.65 · DRIFT (white) 8 s · CYCLE on, LEN 8, RATE 0.1 steps/s · FDBK 80%

## 06 · Function Generator
Audio-rate cycling turns the gray faders into a waveform on the LPF.
Gray set ON → LP · faders alternating 0.2 / 0.9 · DRIFT (gray) 0.05 s · CYCLE on, LEN 4, RATE 250 steps/s · FDBK 60% · RES 45%

## 07 · Broken Courier
Nothing cycles but the Anomaly: a tape hiccup every five seconds.
All sets OFF · CYCLE on, LEN 8, RATE 1.6 steps/s · ANMLY 65%, polarity − · FDBK 70% · MECH 50%

## 08 · Filter Keyboard
The RES-gate synth: MIDI notes C3–G3 play eight filter pitches through the echo.
RES 100% · GATE on · Gray set ON → LP, faders tuned to taste · FDBK 55% · DRY 0 · TP LVL +0 dB

## 09 · Seasick
Slow deep speed mod.
MOD AMT 60% · MOD SPD 0.9 Hz · TIME 500 ms · FDBK 65% · LPF 5 kHz

## 10 · FM Disaster
Motor speed as an FM input.
MOD AMT 70% · MOD SPD 90 Hz · TIME 200 ms · FDBK 50% · Tape IV (keeps composure)

## 11 · Loop Compost
Erase-bypass layering; old takes rot under new ones.
LOOP mode · LOOP 4 s (SYNC for bars) · FDBK 0 · TAPE IN 0 dB · MECH 40%

## 12 · Cassette Synthesizer
Record something in ECHO, switch to PLAY, play positions as pitches.
PLAY mode · White set ON → TM, faders tuned as a scale · 1–8 buttons or MIDI notes

## 13 · Worn Courier
A machine that has lived in a glovebox.
MECH 85% · NOISE 140% · Tape I · OUT DRV 40% · TIME 350 ms · FDBK 60%

## 14 · Chrome Slap
Clean, serviced, type II.
Tape II · MECH 10% · NOISE 60% · TIME 140 ms · FDBK 25% · LPF 12 kHz

## 15 · Metal Master
The most headroom the format allows.
Tape IV · MECH 5% · NOISE 40% · TAPE IN +4 dB · TIME 600 ms · FDBK 45% · Quality ULTRA

## 16 · Wind Down
For the MTR button: kill the motor mid-phrase, let it drag to a stop, release.
TIME 400 ms · FDBK 85% · LPF 4 kHz · MECH 45% — set the motor ramp long, perform MTR
